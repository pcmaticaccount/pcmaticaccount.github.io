2. Reset Your Password

If you have forgotten your password, click on the Forgot Password? link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password securely.

3. Check Your Internet Connection

A slow or unstable internet connection can prevent successful login. Restart your router or switch to a more stable network.

4. Clear Browser Cache and Cookies

Old or corrupted browser data may cause login issues. Clear your browser cache, cookies, and browsing history, then try again.

5. Update Your Browser

Using an outdated browser can cause compatibility problems. Update your browser to the latest version or try using a different browser.

6. Disable Browser Extensions

Some browser extensions, such as ad blockers or VPNs, may interfere with the login process. Temporarily disable them and attempt to log in again.

7. Restart Your Device

A simple device restart can resolve many temporary technical issues. Restart your computer or mobile device and try logging in again.

8. Contact PC Matic Support

If you have tried all the above steps and still cannot log in, contact PC Matic customer support for further assistance.
